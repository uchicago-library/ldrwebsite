University of Chicago Library Digital Repository Acquisitions and Accessions

This is the acquisitions form for the Library Digital Repository (LDR). To start creating an acquisition record, click on "Create an acquisition record" and proceed to enter all the information you have available to you in the correct forms. Remember: if you are adding donors and/or sources and physical media and restriction information, you must add that first before filling out the main form.
